Spaghetti Casserole

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and cook the pasta. Meanwhile in a large skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Stir in spaghetti and tomato sauce and remove from heat.
  2. Drain pasta, combine soup and sour cream and in two 8 inch square baking, dishes, layer half the meat sauce, pasta, soup mixture, cheese. Repeat layers.
  3. Cover and bake 55-65 minutes or until cheese melts.
