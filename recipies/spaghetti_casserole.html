Spaghetti Casserole

Description

Most of us like spaghetti so why not give baked spaghetti a go? It may not suit everyone but a crispy surface and stretchy cheese are two of the best ways to satisfy your comfort food cravings. It takes much longer than simply cooking the spaghetti but I find the effort to be worth it because I prefer pasta dishes baked. It allows the flavors of all the ingredients to sink in.

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and cook the pasta. Meanwhile in a large skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Stir in spaghetti and tomato sauce and remove from heat.
  2. Drain pasta, cream of mushroom combine soup and sour cream and in two 8 inch square baking, dishes, layer half the meat sauce, pasta, soup mixture, cheese. Repeat layers.
  3. Cover and bake 55-65 minutes or until cheese melts.
