Eggnog Icecream

Description

This recipie is good during the holiday season. For eggnog lovers this will add a tasty twist to your winter rituals. Many people may not want to drink eggnog but perhaps they will struggle to say no to icecream. The steps are pretty short and simple but it requires the patience of waiting several hours to a day to fully freeze.

  1. Place 2 cups of eggnog, 1 cup whipping cream, quarter of spiced rum, half a cup of sugar, half teaspoon of nutmeg, half teaspoon cinnamon in a blender.
  2. Process until thickened.
